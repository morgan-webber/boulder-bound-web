Boulder Bound

Four players. One rock. No excuses.

A cooperative physics-based game built in one weekend where four players must work together — and argue constantly — to push a boulder to the goal.

The Concept

What Are We Building?

Boulder Bound is a 4-player cooperative physics game. Players share a single goal: push a massive boulder from the start of a level to the finish zone. There's no shooting, no inventory, no complex systems — just physics, communication, and controlled chaos. One player pushing the wrong side undoes everyone else's work. Success requires coordination; failure is always hilarious.
🪨
Physics-First
The boulder is the game. All fun emerges from how it moves, resists, and reacts to players.
🤝
Cooperative
All 4 players are on the same team. No competitive mechanics — just shared success and shared failure.
🌐
Multiplayer
LAN-based sessions via Unreal's Online Subsystem. Host, find, and join sessions in seconds.
📦
Shippable
Scoped to ship. One polished level, a clean UI loop, and a packaged Windows build by Sunday.
Project Goals

What We're Aiming For

01
Ship a complete game loop Join session → play level → win screen → play again. The full loop, working end-to-end by Sunday.
02
Working LAN multiplayer All 4 devs on the same session simultaneously. Replication solid enough that the boulder is in sync for everyone.
03
Fun in the first 60 seconds A first-time player picks it up, joins a session, and immediately understands what to do — and laughs.
04
Everyone ships something Every dev has clear ownership. Everyone can point to something in the final build and say "I made that."
05
Packaged & playable build A Windows .exe someone could hand to a friend. No editor required to play.
06
Steam-ready foundation Clean enough to list on Steam as a prototype. Proper title screen, credits, and a capsule image.
End Product

What We're Delivering

🎮 Gameplay
  • One fully playable physics-based level with obstacles
  • Boulder that responds to player pushes cooperatively
  • Goal zone with win condition and score/timer
  • Hazards: pits, ramps, bumpers, at least one moving obstacle
  • Player respawn on falling off the map
🌐 Multiplayer
  • LAN session hosting and joining from main menu
  • Up to 4 players simultaneously
  • Server-authoritative physics replication on the boulder
  • Lobby / ready-up screen before match starts
  • Player color differentiation (tell each other apart)
🎨 Presentation
  • Styled main menu with Boulder Bound branding
  • In-game HUD (player count, progress to goal)
  • Win screen with replay option
  • Background music + SFX (roll, impact, win)
  • Credits screen
📦 Build & Distribution
  • Packaged Windows 64-bit build
  • Tested on at least 2 machines before shipping
  • itch.io upload as primary distribution
  • Steam page assets ready (capsule art, description)
  • Basic settings menu (audio volume)
