💡 Environment Tips
  • Use BSP brushes or simple static meshes for fast iteration — don't spend time on complex assets
  • Ramps must be smooth — sharp edges catch the boulder. Use beveled or angled brushes
  • Always test obstacle placement with the actual boulder BP in the scene before finalizing
  • A simple directional light + sky light at correct intensity takes 5 mins and looks dramatically better

Player & Feel
🧍 Dev C
You own how the game feels to play. Movement, camera, push interactions, juice, and feedback are all yours. A good feel can make a simple game feel incredible. Prioritize responsiveness over visual complexity.
Progress 0%
💡 Feel Tips
  • Camera lag (lag speed ~10) makes movement feel polished instantly — enable it in the spring arm component
  • A subtle screen shake on boulder impact costs ~1hr and adds enormous feel
  • Use AddImpulse on the boulder when the player collides — don't rely solely on physics friction
  • 4 colored materials (one per player index) is the minimum needed to tell players apart online
